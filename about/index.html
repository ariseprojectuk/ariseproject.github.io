ARISE means Artistic Recognition for Inpiring Success and Excellence

- we're talking about you!


We are so excited to join you in your screate journey and want to celebrate your wonderful work with the recognition you deserve.

ARISE is the flagship programme of Maker Arts CIC and is designed for creative people to experience and apply themselves to different projects and be rewarded for their efforts and help charitable causes.

Achievement award scheme

There are four challenges

  • Bronze - Identity
  • Silver - The Wonder of Nature
  • Gold - Connection
  • Platinum - It’s so Mystical!


    • You can complete just one, or all four challenges in a time frame that suits you. Create a piece of art, it can be a drawing, painting, made with clay, a sand drawing, pretty much anything that you find creative.


    Find the submission criteria and guidelines at the back of this book and upload your work, sit back and wait for your very special reward in the mail!
